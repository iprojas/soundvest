Frequently asked questions
What is SoundVest and what services does it offer?
SoundVest UG. is a company incorporated in Berlin in 2023, which helps classical string musicians in Europe, accessing and/or acquire the instruments they need for their professional excellence.

The musicians access the instruments by leasing them from the respective owners, which are a network of sound investors represented by Soundvest.

SoundVest offers two lease services:

Instruments in a long-term lease agreement, in which the musician pays for the instrument through a monthly rent fee.

Instruments in a leasing agreement with the option to purchase. In which the musician pays a monthly rent, and after a certain number of years, they can purchase the instrument at a specified price, or continue renting it for another period.
How does the instrument rental process work?

The process of an instrument lease with SoundVest works in the following steps:

  1. A musician approaches SoundVest with a request for a specific instrument they have found in the market, or approaches SoundVest with a need for a yet unspecific instrument that would meet certain characteristics.
  1. Soundvest will evaluate the musician’s request, and their professional, academic, and economic stand. With this information, SoundVest will create a profile of the musician and the instrument.
  1. This profile will be distributed among investors, who will decide or not to invest in the instrument.
  1. If the investors invest in the instrument, SoundVest will engage a luthier to certify the value and quality of the instrument, will purchase the instrument for the investors, and will lease the instrument back to the musician.
  1. With the instrument in their hands, the musician will pay the monthly rent to SoundVest, who will distribute the Rent to the investors.
What kind of instruments does SoundVest help to acquire?
SoundVest helps classical musicians perform with classical string instruments that musicians could find it hard to obtain by themselves on the retail market. Our focus is to help professional and soon-to-be professional musicians play with antique or contemporary instruments for which there are, if any, very few financing alternatives.
How do I apply to play an instrument?
The process of an instrument lease with SoundVest works in the following steps:
  1. You contact SoundVest through the contact form
  2. We will call you back for having more information about what are your needs and where in your career are you.
  3. Soundvest will evaluate the musician’s request, and their professional, academic, and economic stand. With this information, SoundVest will create a profile of the musician and the instrument.
  4. This profile will be distributed among investors, who will decide or not to invest in the instrument.
  5. If the investors invest in the instrument, SoundVest will engage a luthier to certify the value and quality of the instrument, will purchase the instrument for the investors, and will lease the instrument back to the musician.
  6. With the instrument in their hands, the musician will pay the monthly rent to SoundVest, who will distribute the Rent to the investors.
How do you decide who can access an instrument?
Not all musicians are the same, and not all have the same means or needs. What we aim is to evaluate what is the risk for the investors and for SoundVest if the instrument is leased to each musician. And which is the right instrument according to that level of risk of each musician.
Therefore, whenever there may be a case in which a candidate may not have the necessary musical maturity in their career, or the instrument that they want is not rationally adjusted to their career progression, we may decide not to facilitate this process at this time, or we would do it for an instrument the musician initially was not interested in.
What type of instruments are available?
We work only with string instruments: Violin, Viola, Cello, and double bass. These have to meet high-quality standards, either they are antique pieces, or contemporary pieces from reputed luthiers.
What is the cost of renting an instrument?
The cost of renting an instrument will depend on the value of the instrument, the length of the lease contract, and the career profile of each musician. To that, we must add the insurance costs and SoundVest’s service fee.
How long is the rental contract period?
For lease agreements, the minimum rental period is of 1 year. However, if the value of the instrument is within a higher range, the minimum rental period is of 4 years.
Can I buy my rented instrument? How does the process work?
It will depend on the lease contract. Some contracts may offer you the option to purchase the instrument after a period of time. While others may not. It will depend on the type of instrument and its value.
How are investors involved in the process?
Investors are involved in the process in several ways. Primarily by investing in the music instrument that a specific musician needs, secondarily by participating in private concerts and events organized by SoundVest, where they can personally meet the musicians they are supporting.

At SoundVest, our goal is to narrow the space between musicians and music patreons.
What happens if I want to terminate the contract before its end date?
We understand that sometimes the plans change, however, an instrument in the music career is a long commitment. Depending on when would the contract end, you would have to return the instrument to SoundVest, and pay a contract end penalty as indicated in the corresponding lease agreement.
